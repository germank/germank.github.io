I'm a 4th year PhD Student in Computational Linguistics under the direction of Marco Baroni. I work in the area of Distributional Semantics, and more specifically, in understanding the strengths and limitations of distributional models when trying to account for the richness of human conceptual knowledge. To this end, I've explored how these semantic representations can be used in inference.

I'm also interested in how Distributional Semantic Models fit in the big picture of language and of intelligence in general. In this direction, I think language modelling provides a useful task to frame the semantic modelling of words, phrases and sentences. For this reason, I'm also enquiring in how can we produce richer language models that can account for a larger portion of language phenomena.
