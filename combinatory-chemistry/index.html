Combinatory Chemistry

Combinatory Chemistry is an Algorithmic Artificial Chemistry with a minimalistic design, which can display a wide range of emergent structures.

Here, you can find the code and supplementary materials for "Combinatory Chemistry: Towards a Simple Model of Emergent Evolution" by G. Kruszewski and T. Mikolov to be published at the ALIFE 2020 Proceedings.
