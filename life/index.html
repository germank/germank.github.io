LIFE

LIFE is my MSCA project at UPF. The project aims at solving complex linguistic tasks such as pragmatic understanding of a signal by harnessing the exploratory dynamics of an open-ended evolutionary process. For this, I investigate an artificial chemistry derived from my previous work Combinatory Chemistry: Towards a Simple Model of Emergent Evolution"
